The outer is not the inner.
The inner is not the outer.

He is enamored with this desk. So much so that he goes to buy
it because he is afraid someone else will and he will never
feel the same about it. At the same time, he has no need for
this desk.

He enjoys the desk.

He goes to get money from the desk, but the drawer does not 
open. He swings at it with a hatchet trying to get the drawer
to open. It does not open but instead he finds a collection
of papers in a hidden drawer.

The outer is not the inner.
The outer (author swinging hatchet angrily at desk) is not
the inner (author is in love with the desk).
